🏦 Banque Misr: Credit Portfolio & Profitability Analytics

    📌 Project Overview
        This project focuses on an end-to-end analytics solution developed for Banque Misr.
        The primary objective was to investigate a "Growth Paradox": while the bank's customer base and loan portfolio were expanding,
        the net annual profit remained stagnant, failing to recover to its 2018 peak of $6.2M.
        
The analysis identifies the structural gaps in credit strategy and revenue generation that led to this profitability plateau.

    🎯 The Business Problem
            Despite consistent operational growth, the bank faced:
                - Stagnant Profitability: Annual profits fluctuated and stayed below historical peaks.
                - Risk Erosion: A rising 8.33% Default Rate was silently consuming interest margins.
                - Declining Acquisition: A downward trend in new customer onboarding from 2015 to 2024.

    🛠️ Technical Stack
        Power BI: For advanced visualization and interactive reporting.
        DAX: To calculate complex financial KPIs and portfolio health metrics.
        Data Modeling: Segmenting customer behavior and loan performance across multiple dimensions.

    🔍 Analytical Deep Dive
        1. Customer Segmentation
            Size: Analyzed a sample of 1,000 customers.
            Segments: Retail (60%), Mass (30%), and VIP (10%).
            Geography: Identified Mansoura and Alexandria as the highest engagement hubs.
    
        2. Loan Portfolio Analysis ($237M Total)
            Composition: Personal Loans (212), Car Loans (199), and Mortgages (189).
            Status Distribution:
            Active: 65.17%
            Closed: 26.5%
            Defaulted: 8.33% (The primary "profit killer").

    ✅ Strategic Recommendations
        To restore profitability and mitigate risk, the following strategies were proposed:
            - Credit Risk Mitigation: Implement stricter Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratios and re-evaluate scoring models specifically for Personal Loans to bring down the 8.33% default rate.
            - Portfolio Diversification: Shift focus toward Mortgage and SME loans to increase the share of collateralized debt, ensuring long-term stability.
           -  Profitability Recovery: Target the 26.5% of "Closed" loan customers with "Top-Up" loan offers, leveraging their existing positive history to boost income with zero acquisition cost.
            - Geographic Expansion: Launch localized marketing campaigns in high-performing regions like Mansoura and Alexandria to reverse the declining acquisition trend.
